## Formula

- CSR SpMV: `2 * nnz` FLOPs; BiCGSTAB uses two SpMVs per linear iteration.
- Block-Jacobi inverse GEMV apply: `sum_blocks(2*m^2 - m)` FLOPs per apply; BiCGSTAB uses two applies per linear iteration.
- Dot reductions: `8*n - 4` FLOPs per BiCGSTAB linear iteration.
- Vector updates: `8*n` FLOPs for the first BiCGSTAB iteration and `12*n` afterwards.
- Norm checks: `4*n` FLOPs per cuITER solve.
